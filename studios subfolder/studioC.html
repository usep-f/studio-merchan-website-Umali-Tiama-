THE CONSOLE

Only built from premium analog gear, our console is the heart of Studio C's precision sound. It offers unparalleled control over your sound, allowing for meticulous adjustments that bring out the finest details in your tracks.

SURROUND SOUND

A spacious, acoustically treated environment designed to capture the natural reverb and energy of your performance.

AUDIO MONITORS

Complete isolation for vocals and solo instruments, ensuring a pristine signal path with zero bleed.

PRECISE
ANALYSIS

Our control room is equipped with advanced audio analysis tools that allow engineers to scrutinize every aspect of your mix. From spectral analyzers to phase meters, we ensure your tracks meet the highest standards of audio fidelity.

SURGICAL
TOOLS

Studio C boasts a meticulously curated selection of outboard gear designed for precision editing and enhancement. From high-end equalizers to dynamic processors, our tools empower engineers to sculpt your sound with surgical accuracy.

For those seeking the true analog vibe, Studio C offers in-house vinyl cutting services. Our state-of-the-art lathe system allows for the creation of high-quality vinyl records, ensuring that your music is presented with the warmth and depth that only vinyl can provide.
