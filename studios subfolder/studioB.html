THE CONSOLE

An old SSL analog console that imparts warmth and character to those lush recordings, making every track sound radio-ready and giving it some needed mojo.

LIVE ROOM

Small, warm and intimate space perfect for capturing cozy vocals and acoustic instruments with a natural ambiance. Reminiscent of those old Motown recordings.

ISO BOOTH

A cozy isolation booth designed to capture pristine vocal and instrument recordings, minimizing bleed and external noise for maximum clarity.

PRISTINE
RHODES

That chimey Rhodes sound you've been searching for. Our vintage MkI Fender Rhodes electric piano is meticulously maintained to deliver warm, bell-like tones that sit perfectly in any mix. Whether you're after smooth jazz vibes or funky grooves, this classic instrument adds a timeless character to your recordings.

HAMMOND ORGAN

Let the soulful sounds of our vintage Hammond B3 organ elevate your music to new heights. Known for its rich, warm tones and expressive capabilities, this iconic instrument has been a staple in genres ranging from jazz and blues to rock and gospel. Whether you're looking to add depth to your tracks or create captivating solos, our Hammond B3 is ready to inspire your creativity.

A VERY
ANALOG EXPERIENCE

No signs of digital here. Only utilizing vintage analog outboard gear and classic microphones to capture that authentic sound. Our commitment to analog recording techniques ensures that your music retains its warmth, depth, and character, providing a timeless quality that digital simply can't replicate.
