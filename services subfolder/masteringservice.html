STUDIO MASTERING

The final polish before the world hears your art. We provide the critical objective ear and technical precision needed to elevate your mix, ensuring it stands tall against major label releases with clarity, width, and impact.

Book Service

RADIO AND
STREAM READY

We master with the destination in mind. Whether it's the loudness standards of radio or the normalization algorithms of Spotify and Apple Music, we ensure your tracks translate perfectly across every playback system—from earbuds to stadium PAs.

SURROUND EXPERIENCE

Step into the future of audio. As a Dolby Atmos enabled studio, we expand your mix beyond stereo into a fully immersive 3D space. Give your listeners a cinematic experience where every instrument has its own place in the room, enveloping the audience in pure sound.

MASTERS OF
ANALOG

Digital precision meets analog soul. We utilize high-end outboard limiters, EQs, and tape saturation to add that elusive warmth, glue, and harmonic richness to your master. It’s the texture and depth that plugins often strive to emulate but can never quite duplicate.

REVIVING
THE VINYL

Respecting the groove. Vinyl requires a delicate touch and a specific dynamic range. We craft specialized masters optimized for the cutting lathe, managing low-end phase and sibilance to ensure your record sounds warm, punchy, and skip-free on the turntable.
